Construction Invoicing Management

With this module, you can effortlessly create construction invoices for your clients. You can easily specify the quantity and completion percentage from the previous invoice, as well as the quantity and completion percentage for the current invoice. This keeps you always informed about the project's status up to the present moment. The module automatically calculates the value of the current invoice and also allows you to quickly generate the next invoice for the project. Simply duplicate the current invoice and click the button to transfer all quantities and percentages from the previous invoice to the relevant fields in the new invoice, under "Previous Quantities" and "Previous Percentages."
